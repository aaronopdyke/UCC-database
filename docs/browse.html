How to read a full taxonomy string (e.g. MUR+CBH/LWAL/CDN+ERL/H:1/RES)

Segments are separated by “/”: material (MUR unreinforced masonry, CR reinforced concrete, S steel, W wood, ADO adobe, E earth, ME metal — with sub-types after “+”, e.g. +CBH concrete block hollow, +ADO adobe blocks, +PC precast) · lateral load-resisting system (LWAL load-bearing wall, LFM moment frame, LFINF infilled frame, LDUAL dual system, LFBR braced frame, LPB post-and-beam) · seismic design (CDN none, CDL low, CDM moderate, CDH high code level; ERL/ERM/ERH expected ductility low/moderate/high) · height (H:1 one storey, H:1-3 range, H:>4 above four) · occupancy (RES residential, COM commercial, IND industrial, with GEM sub-type numbers such as COM:3 offices or RES:2 multi-unit).
